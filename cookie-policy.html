2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language, login status, and other settings that can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

2.1 Types of Cookies

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device until they expire or are deleted
  • First-Party Cookies: Set directly by our website (dmtools.fun)
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by other services we use (like Google Analytics)

5.2 Browser Controls

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies: Prevents websites from setting cookies (may break functionality)
  • Block third-party cookies: Allows first-party cookies but blocks third-party tracking
  • Clear existing cookies: Deletes all stored cookies from your browser
  • Set cookie preferences: Configure which types of cookies to allow
Important: Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features of our service, including logging in and saving your preferences.

5.3 Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data
