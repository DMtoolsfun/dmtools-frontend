Gravity Runner Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DMTOOLS-FUN LLC handles information in connection with the Gravity Runner mobile game.

1. Who We Are

Gravity Runner is published by DMTOOLS-FUN LLC. If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at abbey_dale@dmtools.fun.

2. Information We May Collect

Gravity Runner is designed to be played without creating an account. We do not require players to submit names, usernames, messages, or other account information to use the game.

Depending on the services enabled in the app, the game or its service providers may process limited technical information such as:

  • device and app identifiers
  • advertising identifiers
  • app version, device type, operating system, language, and basic diagnostic information
  • gameplay and usage events needed to operate, improve, measure, or support the app
  • purchase or entitlement status where applicable, such as ad-removal ownership or restore checks

3. Advertising, Analytics, and Third-Party Services

Gravity Runner may use third-party services to support advertising, analytics, app performance, crash analysis, store distribution, and in-app purchase functionality. These providers may process device-level or app-level information according to their own privacy policies.

If ads are enabled in the version of the app you use, advertising partners may use advertising identifiers or similar data to deliver, measure, and improve ads.

4. How We Use Information

  • operate and maintain Gravity Runner
  • deliver and measure advertising
  • support purchases, restores, or entitlements where available
  • diagnose crashes, bugs, and performance issues
  • improve gameplay, balance, usability, and reliability
  • comply with legal obligations and protect the app from abuse or misuse

5. Sharing

We do not sell player-submitted personal information through Gravity Runner. Information may be processed or shared with service providers that help us operate the app, such as hosting, analytics, advertising, app store, or payment-related providers, only as needed to support the app and related services.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate, secure, support, and improve Gravity Runner, or as required by law. Some local game data may remain on your device until you delete the app or clear app data.

7. Your Choices

  • You can stop all collection by uninstalling the app.
  • You may be able to reset or limit advertising identifiers through your device settings.
  • You may contact us at abbey_dale@dmtools.fun with privacy-related questions.

8. Children

Gravity Runner is a general-audience game and is not intentionally designed to collect personal information directly from children through account creation, messaging, or user-generated content features.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information associated with the operation of the app, but no method of transmission, storage, or processing is completely secure.

10. International Processing

Depending on the services used to operate Gravity Runner, information may be processed in countries other than your own.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the effective date above and publish the revised version at https://dmtools.fun.
