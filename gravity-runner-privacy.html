Gravity Runner Privacy Policy

Publisher: DMtools.fun LLC
Contact: admin@dmtools.fun
Effective Date: May 2026

Overview

Gravity Runner is a general audience arcade game published by DMtools.fun LLC. The app does not require an account.

Local Gameplay Data

Gravity Runner may store gameplay progress and preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Best score
  • Current level
  • Difficulty setting
  • Sound setting
  • Retry progress
  • Ad removal entitlement state

Ads

Gravity Runner may show ads using third-party ad services. Rewarded ads are optional and user-initiated, meaning you choose whether to watch them in exchange for an in-game reward or benefit.

In-App Purchases

Gravity Runner may offer in-app purchases, such as Remove Ads, through supported app stores and billing providers. DMtools.fun LLC does not directly receive or store your full payment card details.

Third-Party Services

Third-party services used for ads, app store billing, diagnostics, or platform features may process device, ad interaction, purchase, diagnostic, or app usage data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Children's Privacy

Gravity Runner is a general audience arcade game and is not designed to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so we can review the request.

Contact

For privacy or support requests, contact DMtools.fun LLC at admin@dmtools.fun.
