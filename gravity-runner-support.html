Gravity Runner Support

Contact: admin@dmtools.fun

Remove Ads / Restore Purchases

If Remove Ads is not active after purchase, confirm that you are signed in to the same app store account used for the purchase and use the store's restore purchase option when available. If the issue continues, contact support.

Rewarded Ads

Rewarded ads are optional and user-initiated. Availability may depend on your device, region, network connection, ad provider inventory, or app store build.

Progress Reset

Gravity Runner may store progress locally on your device. Clearing app data, uninstalling the app, changing devices, or certain system cleanup tools may reset local progress and settings.

Bug Reports

To report a bug, email admin@dmtools.fun. Please include:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • Store used to install the app
  • What happened and what you expected instead
  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if available

Privacy

Review the Gravity Runner Privacy Policy for information about local gameplay data, ads, purchases, and third-party services.
