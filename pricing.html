What is the difference between a subscription and a pack?

A subscription gives you recurring reply capacity and plan features. A pack is a one-time purchase that adds extra reply balance.

Can I buy a pack without a subscription?

Yes. Packs can work on their own or as extra capacity on top of a subscription.

Do I need an account to checkout?

No. Lemon Squeezy collects your email during checkout. If you are already signed in, DMTools securely connects the purchase to your current account automatically.
