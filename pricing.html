Frequently Asked Questions

How does the hybrid model work?
If you're on a paid plan (Pro, Business, or Enterprise), the Chrome extension uses your monthly response allowance. Free users need to purchase response packs to use the extension.
Can I use some responses for batch and some for extension?
Yes! If you're a Pro or Business subscriber, your monthly responses work for BOTH the batch processor and the Chrome extension. Use them however you want - 100% batch, 100% extension, or any mix!
Why is Enterprise unlimited for the extension?
Enterprise plans include 25,000 batch responses PLUS unlimited inline extension responses. This means your batch allowance is completely separate - heavy extension users never run out!
Should I buy packs or subscribe?
If you only use the extension occasionally (< 50 responses/month), packs are great. If you're a regular user, Pro subscription ($59/month) gives you 1,000 responses PLUS the batch processor - much better value!
Do response packs expire?
Inactive packs never expire! Once you activate a pack, you have 12 months to use all responses. You can only have one active pack at a time.
