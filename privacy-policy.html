Privacy Policy

How DMTools.fun collects, uses, protects, and discloses information for the website, web app, Queue, Voice, and Chrome Extension.

Effective Date: April 25, 2026 Last Updated: April 25, 2026

1. Introduction

DMTools.fun LLC ("DMTools.fun," "we," "our," or "us") provides AI-assisted message drafting tools that help users capture messages, generate draft replies, and stay in flow. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, retain, and protect information when you use our website, web application, Queue, Voice features, response packs, and Chrome Extension.

By accessing or using DMTools.fun, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy should be read together with our Terms of Service, Refund Policy, and Cookie Policy.

Important human-review notice: DMTools.fun generates draft responses only. DMTools.fun does not send messages for users. Users must review, edit, approve, and manually send any communication themselves.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to DMTools.fun services, including the DMTools.fun website, web application, Queue, Voice, Response Packs, account pages, billing flows, and the DMTools Chrome Extension available through the Chrome Web Store.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Account Information

  • Email address, account credentials, and authentication details.
  • Optional profile information, saved settings, tone preferences, and account configuration.
  • Subscription plan, billing status, invoices, and payment-related records processed by our payment provider.

3.2 Usage and Technical Data

  • Feature usage, queue activity, response generation counts, template usage, plan limits, and performance events.
  • Device type, browser type, operating system, referring page, approximate location derived from IP address, session events, and diagnostic logs.
  • Error logs, abuse-prevention signals, security events, and service reliability metrics.

3.3 Message Content and Drafting Inputs

When you use DMTools.fun to generate a draft, the text you provide may be processed temporarily to create the requested output. DMTools.fun does not permanently store message content unless you intentionally save it as a template, preset, queue item, account preference, support attachment, or other saved feature.

Generated outputs may be temporarily displayed, cached, or logged as necessary to provide the service, troubleshoot issues, enforce abuse protections, and maintain reliability.

3.4 Chrome Extension Content Access

The DMTools Chrome Extension accesses content from the currently active webpage only when you intentionally activate the extension or otherwise use its inline reply features. The extension does not monitor browsing history, collect keystrokes, access background tabs for unrelated browsing activity, or automatically send replies.

3.5 Sources of Information

  • Information you provide directly through the website, app, extension, Queue, Voice, forms, and support requests.
  • Information created through your use of the service, including usage events and generated draft activity.
  • Payment and subscription information from authorized payment processors.
  • Analytics, hosting, security, and infrastructure systems used to operate the service.

3.6 Sensitive Personal Information

We do not intentionally collect highly sensitive personal information unless you choose to include it in content you submit. We may process limited data that may be considered sensitive in certain jurisdictions, such as IP address, account login data, security logs, and precise content you voluntarily submit for drafting. We use this information only to provide, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the service, or as otherwise permitted by law.

4. AI Functionality, Human Review, and User Control

DMTools.fun is a drafting assistant. AI-generated outputs are suggestions only and may be inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, offensive, unsuitable, or legally inappropriate for a particular use.

  • DMTools.fun does not send direct messages, emails, social messages, or other communications on behalf of users.
  • Users must manually review, edit, approve, copy, insert, export, or send any generated response.
  • Users remain solely responsible for any message they choose to send or use.
  • DMTools.fun does not use AI outputs to make legal, financial, employment, credit, housing, healthcare, or similarly significant decisions about users.

5. How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve DMTools.fun.
  • Generate AI-assisted draft responses at your request.
  • Manage accounts, authentication, subscriptions, billing, plan limits, and customer support.
  • Remember user preferences, templates, tone settings, and saved workflows.
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, spam, security incidents, scraping, excessive usage, and Terms violations.
  • Analyze performance, usage trends, reliability, and product experience.
  • Send service notices, security alerts, policy updates, support responses, and promotional messages where permitted.
