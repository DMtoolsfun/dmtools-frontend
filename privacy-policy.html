How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Effective Date: December 16, 2024
Last Updated: December 16, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to DMTools.fun! We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered universal unlimited batch response draft generator assistant service operated by Dale Abbey.

Chrome Extension Notice

This Privacy Policy applies to the DMTools.fun website, web application, and theDMTools Chrome Extension available through the Google Chrome Web Store.

Quick Summary: We collect minimal personal information, process your messages in real-time without permanent storage, and never sell your data to third parties. Your privacy is our priority.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (required for account creation and communication)
  • Name (optional, for personalization)
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Subscription plan and billing information (processed by Lemon Squeezy, our authorized payment processor)

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use our service:

  • Number of AI responses generated
  • Response tone preferences and template usage
  • Feature usage patterns and performance metrics
  • Login times, session duration, and frequency of use
  • Device information (browser type, operating system, IP address)
  • Error logs and technical diagnostic information

2.3 Message Content (Temporary Processing Only)

Important: We do NOT permanently store your message content. Messages are processed in real-time through OpenAI's API and immediately discarded after generating responses.
  • Original messages you input for response generation
  • Context information you provide (processed temporarily)
  • Custom response templates you create (stored until deleted)
  • Generated responses (temporarily cached for your session)

Browser Content Access

The DMTools Chrome Extension accesses content from the currently active webpage only when the user explicitly activates the extension to generate a response.The extension does not monitor browsing activity, collect browsing history, or access background tabs.

2.4 Analytics and Cookies

We use Google Analytics and cookies for:

Google Analytics is used on the DMTools.fun website only. The DMTools Chrome Extension does not use analytics tracking beyond essential error logging required for functionality.

  • Website performance monitoring and improvement
  • User experience optimization and feature usage analytics
  • Essential cookies for login and session management
  • Preference cookies to remember your settings
