2. General Refund Policy

2.1 Standard Policy

Due to the digital nature of our service and instant access to AI-generated responses, refunds are provided under specific circumstances outlined below. This policy is designed to be fair to both our customers and our business operations.

Our Free Forever plan requires no payment and therefore no refunds are applicable. Users can cancel or stop using the service at any time without any charges or obligations.

2.3 7-Day Free Trial

Our Pro and Business plans include a 7-day free trial period:

5. Refund Eligibility Criteria

5.1 Eligible Refund Reasons

Refunds may be approved for the following reasons:

  • Technical issues preventing service use (after troubleshooting attempts)
  • Service not performing as described in our marketing materials
  • Duplicate charges or billing errors on our part
  • Service downtime exceeding 48 consecutive hours
  • Major feature removal without adequate notice
  • Inability to cancel subscription due to technical issues on our platform

5.2 Non-Eligible Refund Reasons

Refunds will generally not be provided for:

  • Change of mind after the satisfaction guarantee period
  • Lack of use or forgetting about the subscription
  • Dissatisfaction with AI-generated content quality (inherent AI limitations)
  • Account termination due to Terms of Service violations
  • Requests made after the applicable guarantee period
  • Issues caused by user error or misuse of the service
  • Refunds requested for previous billing cycles beyond current period

6. Refund Process

6.1 How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact Us: Email admin@dmtools.fun with "Refund Request" in the subject line
  2. Provide Information: Include your account email, reason for refund, and relevant details
  3. Response Time: We'll respond within 48 hours (typically within 24 hours)
  4. Review Process: We may ask for additional information or attempt to resolve issues first
  5. Decision: We'll notify you of our decision within 5 business days

6.2 Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed through lemon squeezy within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds appear in your original payment method within 3-10 business days
  • Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider
  • We'll provide you with a refund confirmation and tracking information

6.3 Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds:

  • Prorated refunds based on unused time in billing period
  • Service credits for future use instead of cash refunds
  • Account upgrades or plan changes as alternative solutions
  • Extended trial periods for additional evaluation time

7.1 Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues:
