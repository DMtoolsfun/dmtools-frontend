1. Overview

This Refund Policy applies to purchases made from DMTools.fun LLC ("DMTools.fun," "we," "our," or "us"), including subscriptions, renewals, response packs, usage credits, and other digital products or services. By purchasing or using a paid DMTools.fun service, you agree to this Refund Policy.

Digital service notice: DMTools.fun provides immediate access to digital software, AI-assisted drafting, credits, and account features. Because access begins immediately after purchase, refunds are limited as described below and as required by applicable law.

2. Free Plans and Trials

  • Free plans do not require payment and are not eligible for refunds.
  • Trial users must cancel before the trial ends to avoid being charged.
  • Failure to cancel before a billing date does not automatically qualify for a refund.
  • One free trial or promotional trial may be available per user, email, payment method, or account household, at our discretion.

Except where required by law or expressly approved by DMTools.fun, subscription fees are final once charged. Users are responsible for reviewing plan terms, renewal dates, and cancellation settings before purchasing or renewing.

Subscription renewals are non-refundable. You may cancel future renewals through your account settings or by contacting support before the renewal date. Canceling stops future billing but does not automatically refund past charges.

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable except where required by law or expressly approved by DMTools.fun in writing:

  • Top-up message packs, response packs, usage credits, and other digital consumables.
  • Partially used subscription periods after the applicable refund window.
  • Expired billing periods, previous months, or prior renewal cycles.
  • Promotional, discounted, beta, founder, or special-price purchases unless the promotion says otherwise.
  • Accounts terminated or suspended for Terms of Service violations, abuse, fraud, chargeback misuse, or prohibited conduct.

5. Eligible Refund Reasons

Refunds may be granted at our discretion for:

  • Verified duplicate charges or billing errors caused by DMTools.fun or our payment processor.
  • Documented technical failures that prevent access to core paid functionality and cannot be resolved after reasonable troubleshooting.
  • Extended platform outage exceeding 48 consecutive hours affecting paid core functionality.
  • Material service discontinuation where unused prepaid access remains, unless governed by separate custom terms.

6. Non-Eligible Refund Reasons

  • Dissatisfaction with AI-generated draft quality, tone, wording, or usefulness.
  • Change of mind, lack of use, forgetting to cancel, or no longer needing the service.
  • User error, incorrect plan selection, misunderstanding features, or failure to review plan details.
  • Internet, browser, device, third-party platform, or extension conflicts outside our reasonable control.
  • Requests submitted after the applicable refund window.
  • Requests involving prior billing cycles, except for verified billing errors.

7. Refund Request Process

  1. Email admin@dmtools.fun with the subject line "Refund Request."
  2. Include your account email, purchase date, plan, charge amount, and reason for the request.
  3. Provide screenshots, error details, invoice IDs, or other evidence if relevant.
  4. We will generally respond within 48 hours and aim to make a decision within 5 business days.

We may attempt troubleshooting, offer service credits, provide an account adjustment, or request additional information before approving or denying a refund.

8. Processing of Approved Refunds

Approved refunds are processed through our payment provider to the original payment method where possible. Payment processors and banks may require additional time before funds appear. Processing time commonly ranges from 5 to 10 business days, but may vary by payment method, bank, country, processor, or fraud review.

9. Partial Refunds, Credits, and Account Adjustments

At our discretion, we may offer partial refunds, service credits, plan adjustments, trial extensions, or replacement credits instead of a cash refund. Credits have no cash value and may expire if not used within the stated period.

10. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

Please contact us before initiating a chargeback. Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute without first giving us a reasonable opportunity to resolve the issue may result in account suspension, service termination, loss of promotional pricing, collection of unpaid amounts, and submission of evidence to the payment processor or card network.

We reserve the right to dispute chargebacks and provide transaction records, account activity, login records, usage data, communications, policy acceptance records, and other evidence permitted by law.
