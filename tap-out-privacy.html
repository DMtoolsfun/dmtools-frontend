Tap-Out Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Accounts

Tap-Out does not require an account. DMTools.fun LLC does not directly collect account or profile data through Tap-Out.

Local Device Data

Tap-Out may store local data on your device to run the game and remember your preferences, including:

  • Player name
  • Local leaderboard and high scores
  • Difficulty setting
  • Sound or mute preference
  • Local gameplay preferences and progress

Advertising

Tap-Out uses Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads to show ads.

Ad partners may collect data such as advertising identifiers, device information, diagnostics, ad interactions, and approximate location depending on Google/AdMob behavior and your device, privacy, and account settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact DMTools.fun LLC at admin@dmtools.fun.
