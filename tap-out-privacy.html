Tap-Out Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Accounts And Contact Information

Tap-Out does not require an account, login, or user profile. Tap-Out does not provide chat, social posting, or user-to-user messaging.

DMTools.fun LLC does not knowingly collect personal contact information directly through Tap-Out. If you email support, we use the information you provide to respond to your request.

Local Device Data

Tap-Out may store local data on your device to run the game and remember your preferences, including:

  • Player name
  • Local leaderboard and high scores
  • Difficulty setting
  • Sound or mute preference
  • Local gameplay preferences and progress

This local gameplay data is stored on your device and is not sent to DMTools.fun LLC servers by Tap-Out. Local player name, initials, leaderboard, and high score data remain local unless future online services are added and this policy is updated.

Advertising

Tap-Out contains ads. The app uses Google AdMob and the Google Mobile Ads SDK through the installed Capacitor AdMob plugin to display banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. An App Open ad unit may be configured for future support, but Tap-Out does not display App Open ads in the current plugin version.

Google Mobile Ads SDK and related ad services may collect and share data for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, and ad delivery. This can include IP address, user product interactions such as ad views or taps, diagnostics, and device or account identifiers such as the Android advertising ID, depending on Google SDK behavior and your device, privacy, and account settings.

Where required or supported, Tap-Out uses Google/AdMob consent messaging so users can make applicable privacy choices before ads are requested.

Device Permissions

Tap-Out does not request access to contacts, photos, videos, files, microphone, camera, or precise device location. The Android app uses internet access so the game can load and show ads.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact DMTools.fun LLC at admin@dmtools.fun or visit the Tap-Out support page.
